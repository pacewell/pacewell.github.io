Pace

See your finishing time before you start.

A calm, open-source pomodoro focus timer for Chrome. Plan the whole run — breaks and all — and Pace shows the clock time you’ll finish before you begin. Then it enforces the recovery breaks, so you keep a working pace you can actually sustain.
